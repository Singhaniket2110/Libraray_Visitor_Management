How to Use This System

Complete guide to using the Library Visitor Management System

For Students

1

Entry Process

  • Click on "Student Entry Portal" on homepage
  • Fill in your Name and Roll Number
  • Select your Education Level:
    • JC (Junior College): Select year (FYJC/SYJC) and stream (Science/Arts/Commerce)
    • UG (Undergraduate): Select course and year (First/Second/Third)
    • PG (Postgraduate): Select course and year (First/Second)
  • Choose your Purpose of Visit (Reading, Book Issue, Reference, etc.)
  • Click "Submit Entry"
  • Your entry time will be recorded automatically!
2

Exit Process

  • When leaving, click on "Student Exit" on homepage
  • Enter your Roll Number
  • The system will show your details automatically
  • Click "Mark Exit" to complete your visit
  • Your exit time will be recorded!
3

Check Status

  • You can check if you're currently in the library by entering your Roll Number
  • The system shows your entry time, purpose, and current status
  • If you've already exited, it will show your complete visit history

Important Tips for Students

Always mark your exit when leaving the library

Use your correct Roll Number for accurate tracking

Fill all required fields marked with *

Select the appropriate purpose for your visit

If you face any issues, contact the library desk

Your visit history is stored securely for future reference
